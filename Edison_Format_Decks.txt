## **DECK BALANCE ANALYSIS**

**LIGHTSWORN SYNCHRO CONTROL:**
- Strengths: Powerful late game, mill-based card advantage, defensive options
- Weaknesses: Inconsistent early game, vulnerable to aggressive rushes
- Key plays: Set up Judgment Dragon, synchro toolbox responses

**QUICKDRAW PLANT SYNCHRO:**
- Strengths: Fast synchro access, consistent opening hands, board pressure
- Weaknesses: Less late game power, vulnerable to disruption
- Key plays: Multi-synchro turns, plant engine setup

**MATCHUP ANALYSIS:**
- Estimated 50-50 overall matchup
- Lightsworn favored in longer games (60-40)
- Quickdraw Plant favored in aggressive games (60-40)
- Both decks can steal games through explosive plays

**Key Edison Format Rules:**
- March 2010 banlist (Heavy Storm limited, Pot of Greed banned, etc.)
- Priority system with pre-2011 rulings
- Synchro summoning is primary extra deck mechanic
- Cards released up to Absolute Powerforce legal

**Collection Analysis:**
- Total Edison-legal cards found: 7,916 cards
